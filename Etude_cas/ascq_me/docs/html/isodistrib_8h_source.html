12  by peptide mass fingerprinting. Its scoring is based on spectral correlation
13  between an experimental MS spectrum and theoretical MS spectra. The experimental
14  spectrum is a mass spectrum without mono isotopic mass extraction. Theoretical
15  spectra are generated from proteins in FASTA format thanks to an algorithm
16  based on Fast Fourier Transform.
